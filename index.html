About me

I am a sophomore undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, majoring in Electrical engineering and pursuing a minor in Systems and Control Engineering. I am very much interested in control systems, autonomous robotics, condensed matter physics and particle physics. My other interests include Astrophysics, cosmology, logic puzzles and in general applied physics and mathematics. I enjoy badminton, cycling and swimming in my free time. I love fiction, and occasionally divulge in the pleasure of blogging. You can usually find me at my best in the Control Engineering or the Physics department.
I am currently the convener for Maths and Physics Club, IIT Bombay. Do check out the club website, and some of the blogs I've written here.
