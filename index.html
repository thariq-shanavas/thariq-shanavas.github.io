About me

I recieved my PhD in Physics from the University of Colorado, Boulder, USA. I worked with Prof. Juliet Gopinath at the Optics and Photonics Research Group.

My primary expertise is in computational and experimental optics, in particular non-linear processes in integrated photonic devices. I have a fair amount of experience with circuit design and debugging, FPGA programming, and Linux systems. I have worked in clean rooms for on-chip optical device fabrication. My PhD research produced two patents and several publications. I maintain an up-to-date list of publications and expertise here.
