About me

I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, with a major in Electrical engineering and a minor in Physics. I am currently a graduate student in Physics at the University of Colorado, Boulder, USA.

I read fiction, build and fly drones, and read about Astrophysics and cosmology in my free time. Hobbyist tinkerer, and I'm always working on some pet project; Check this out- Open Source Voice Assistant with Face recognition. Also, Relevant XKCD.

I think Physics is fun, and want Physics to be fun for everyone! I am privileged to have led a team of six sophomore students as the manager of the Maths and Physics Club at IIT Bombay in the term 2017-18, tending to a community of over 600 enthusiasts in the campus. Do check out the club website and some of the blogs I've written here.
