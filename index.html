About me

I am a senior undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, pursuing a major in Electrical engineering minor in Physics. I am interested in photonics and experimental particle physics, in particular the electronic backend of detectors and accelerator technology.

I like to read about Astrophysics and cosmology, play around with logic puzzles and tinker with RC drones in my free time. I love fiction, and occasionally divulge in the pleasure of blogging.
I am privileged to have led a team of six sophomore students as the manager of the Maths and Physics Club in the term 2017-18, tending to a community of over 600 enthusiasts in the campus and over 7000 online. Do check out the club website and some of the blogs I've written here.
