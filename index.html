About me

I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, with a major in Electrical engineering and a minor in Physics. I am currently pursuing a PhD in Physics at the University of Colorado, Boulder, USA. I work with Prof. Juliet Gopinath at the Optics and Photonics Research Group.

My primary expertise is in computational and experimental optics, in particular super-resolution microscopy and non-linear processes in integrated photonic devices. I have a fair amount of experience with circuit design and debugging, FPGA programming, and Linux systems. I have worked in clean rooms for on-chip optical device fabrication.
