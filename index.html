About me

I am a third year undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, majoring in Electrical engineering and pursuing a minor in Physics. I am very much interested in experimental particle physics, in particular the electronic backend of detectors and accelerator technology. My other interests include Astrophysics, cosmology, logic puzzles and in general applied physics and mathematics. I enjoy badminton, cycling and swimming in my free time. I love fiction, and occasionally divulge in the pleasure of blogging.
I am currently the manager of Maths and Physics Club, IIT Bombay. I lead a team of six conveners to tend to a community of over 600 enthusiasts in the campus, and over 7000 online. Do check out the club website and some of the blogs I've written here.
